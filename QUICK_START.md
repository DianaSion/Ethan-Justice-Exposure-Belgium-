Student guidance centers in Belgium that provide support but can escalate to child welfare.

### SDJ (Service Droit de la Jeunesse)
Youth rights service in Belgium that handles child welfare cases.

### OOOC Ter Heide
An independent child diagnostic center that assessed Ethan.
