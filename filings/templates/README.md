# Legal Templates Directory

This directory contains reusable templates for legal filings, letters, and communications.

## Available Templates

### 1. Expert Citation Template
**File:** `expert_citation_template.md`

**Purpose:** Guide for incorporating medical and psychological expert opinions into legal filings

**Use cases:**
- Citing OOOC Ter Heide diagnostic
- Citing MPK/Vleugel K cPTSD diagnosis
- Citing ER medical records
- Building causation arguments

**Includes:**
- Citation structure
- Methodology descriptions
- Findings presentation
- Conclusion quotations
- Legal argument linkages

### 2. Complaint Template
**File:** `../complaints/complaint_template.md`

**Purpose:** General structure for complaints addressing institutional misconduct

**Covers:**
- Procedural timing defects
- Evidence access issues
- Expert consensus disregard
- Relief requested

### 3. Record Access Motion
**File:** `../complaints/record_access_motion.md`

**Purpose:** Motion for access to files and evidence preservation

**Addresses:**
- Audio evidence access
- Chain of custody
- Missing documents
- Preservation orders

## Using Templates

### Customization Required
**All templates require:**
- ✅ Jurisdiction-specific legal citations
- ✅ Current case information
- ✅ Accurate dates and references
- ✅ Proper recipient information
- ✅ Signature blocks completed
- ✅ Exhibit lists verified

### Legal Review
**Before using any template:**
1. Review with legal counsel familiar with Belgian law
2. Verify procedural requirements
3. Ensure all required elements present
4. Confirm proper court/authority addressed
5. Check filing deadlines and format requirements

### Adaptation Guidelines
**When customizing:**
- Maintain overall structure
- Add jurisdiction-specific citations
- Include all required procedural elements
- Attach proper exhibits
- Follow local formatting rules
- Use appropriate legal terminology

### Not Legal Advice
**IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER:**

These templates are provided for informational and organizational purposes only. They do not constitute legal advice and should not be filed without review by qualified legal counsel familiar with:
- Belgian juvenile law
- Civil procedure
- Human rights law (ECHR, UNCRC)
- Local court rules
- Privacy and data protection law

## Contributing Templates

If you have legal expertise and wish to contribute additional templates:

1. **Open an issue** describing the proposed template
2. **Provide structure** and key elements
3. **Include explanations** for each section
4. **Add usage guidelines**

- Specific enough to be useful
