# Appeals Directory

This directory contains appeal briefs, decisions, and related materials from appellate proceedings.

## Contents

### Appeal Briefs
- Written arguments submitted to appellate courts
- Supporting memoranda
- Legal research and citations

### Appellate Decisions
- Court decisions on appeals
- Orders granting or denying appeals
- Remand instructions

### Supporting Materials
- Transcripts of lower court proceedings
- Appendices of exhibits
- Reply briefs

## Organization

Files should be organized chronologically and named clearly:
- `YYYY-MM-DD_brief_[subject].pdf`
- `YYYY-MM-DD_decision_[outcome].pdf`
- `YYYY-MM-DD_reply_brief.pdf`

## Privacy & Redaction

All materials must be redacted per `PRIVACY.md`:
- ✅ Minor's full name and identifying details
- ✅ Non-public individuals
- ✅ Sensitive personal information
- ✅ Metadata stripped

## Cross-References

Appeals should reference:
- Original lower court rulings in `/evidence/04_juvenile_court_SDJ/`
- Timeline entries in `/analysis/timeline.md`
- Contradictions in `/analysis/contradictions.md`
- Expert evidence in `/evidence/05_medical_psych/`

## Legal Context

Appeals have been successful in:
- Reversing out-of-home placement extensions
- Incorporating expert assessments
- Addressing procedural defects
- Preventing actual removal despite orders

Document both successful and unsuccessful appeals for complete case history.
